About PCGT

Public Concern for Governance Trust (PCGT) was established in 2002 by Padma Bhushan Julio Francis Ribeiro and Dr. Ram Kumar Anand with the vision of strengthening civic engagement for transparent and accountable governance. The trust works tirelessly to promote constitutional values and democratic participation among citizens, especially the youth.

Through various initiatives including the Model Youth Parliament, PCGT has reached thousands of students across India, creating a generation of constitutionally aware and civically responsible citizens.

Founding Trustees

  • Julio Francis Ribeiro (Padma Bhushan)
  • Dr. Ram Kumar Anand

Vision

A corruption-free India with transparent and accountable governance at all levels.

PGCL & SVKM

Pravin Gandhi College of Law is a premier institution under the SVKM trust, established in 2002. The college has earned A++ NAAC accreditation and is permanently affiliated to the University of Mumbai. It is approved by the Bar Council of India and recognized by the UGC.

SVKM (Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal) is a charitable trust established in 1934, running over 35 educational institutions across Mumbai.

The Event

Model Youth Parliament

The Model Youth Parliament is an experiential learning simulation that brings together over 400 students from across India. Participants engage in parliamentary procedures, policy debates, and governance simulations across 9 dynamic committees.

The 7th edition in 2026 continues the tradition of developing constitutional literacy, reasoned debate, and leadership skills among India's youth.

Theme 2026

Samvidhan · Sadbhavana · Samarpan

Samvidhan (Constitution): Embodying constitutional morality and the values enshrined in our founding document - justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity.

Sadbhavana (Goodwill): Representing ethical harmony, fraternity, and the spirit of mutual respect that binds our diverse nation together.

Samarpan (Dedication): Reflecting complete dedication to public service and the pursuit of good governance for all citizens.

"The Model Youth Parliament embodies the vision of Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam - empowering youth to become active participants in India's democratic journey."
